ABOUT OUR SCHOOL

Our History

DePaul Austin Academy was established in the year 2017. And since then, have grown to uphold firm Christian values and morals in the pupils and staff. The school began at a preschool level and has progressively grown with time in terms of population and facilities. In the 8-4-4 system, DePaul Austin Academy has had two class 8 KCPE candidate classes. The first one was in 2021 which performed exemplary well and attained a mean score of 395.4 marks. in 2022, there was a significant improvement, and candidate class 8 scored a mean grade of 410.06 marks. We are pleased to be among the early adopters of the 2-6-3-3 CBC curriculum and already graduated and enrolled our first batch of junior secondary students this year 2023. In accordance with the expectations of the Ministry of Education going forward, we are fully equipped, well-prepared, and able to manage the program.
